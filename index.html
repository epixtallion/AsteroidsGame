ASTEROIDS

Use WASD to move. B for hyperspace. Shoot down the asteroids with SPACEBAR.
You get three lives indicated on the top-right corner. Your ammo is also indicated under it.
Each time you clear all the asteroids, the map gets harder but you get more ammo. Running out of ammo inflicts a penalty in which you cannot shoot for 2.5 seconds. Good luck!
For debug, press Q.
